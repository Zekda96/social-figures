# Sources

## CEO Compensation

- **Equilar / Associated Press CEO Pay Study 2025** — median S&P 500 CEO total compensation: $17.1M (2024 fiscal year), up 9.7% from $15.6M in 2023; median CEO-to-worker pay ratio 192:1.
  https://www.equilar.com/reports/118-equilar-associated-press-ceo-pay-study-2025.html

- **Harvard Law School Forum on Corporate Governance — S&P 500 CEO Compensation Trends (Feb 2026)** — confirms 2023 median at $16.1M, 2024 at ~$17M; notes 5% year-over-year growth.
  https://corpgov.law.harvard.edu/2026/02/04/sp-500-ceo-compensation-trends-2/

## Household Income by Education

- **U.S. Census Bureau — Income in the United States: 2024 (P60-286, Sep 2025)** — median household income $83,730 overall; households headed by someone with a bachelor's degree or higher: $132,700.
  https://www.census.gov/library/publications/2025/demo/p60-286.html

- **U.S. Census Bureau — Education and Income (Sep 2025)** — income gap between college-degree and high-school-only householders; confirms bachelor's+ household median at $132,700 for 2024.
  https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2025/09/education-and-income.html

- **NCES — Annual Earnings by Educational Attainment (2024)** — individual full-time worker medians for 25–34 year olds: master's or higher $80,200 vs. bachelor's $66,600 (2022 earnings).
  https://nces.ed.gov/programs/coe/indicator/cba/annual-earnings

- **BLS — Usual Weekly Earnings by Educational Attainment, 2024 (Table 37b)** — individual median weekly earnings: master's ~$1,930/wk (~$100K/yr), doctoral ~$2,040/wk (~$106K/yr), professional ~$2,129/wk (~$111K/yr).
  https://www.bls.gov/cps/cpsaat37b.pdf

  https://www.federalreserve.gov/publications/october-2023-changes-in-us-family-finances-from-2019-to-2022.htm
